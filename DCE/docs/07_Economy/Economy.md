# DCE Economy (AI-Only Simulation)

**Status:** Accepted
**Version:** 1.1
**Owner:** Datunkownrazzy
**Dependencies:** Integration Manager, Organizations, AI Director

---

## Purpose

The `dce-economy` service manages the financial status of AI-controlled criminal organizations. Because these entities are strictly local/AI-driven, this service does not interface with player banking or inventory resources. Instead, it maintains the authoritative internal ledger of **Operational Capital** used exclusively by the AI Director to govern behavior, scaling, and organizational "power." The Organizations Service may expose a convenience field such as `money` for read-model consumption, but the Economy service remains the single owner of the financial state.

---

## The AI Financial Model

Since there are no player bank accounts, the Economy service tracks two virtual states:

*   **Illicit Funds (Dirty):** Cumulative wealth generated by successful AI-led incidents (e.g., smuggling, extortion). This data is stored in the Organization's runtime memory.
*   **Operating Budget (Clean):** A calculated percentage of Illicit Funds that the AI Director "unlocks" for use. This represents money that has been "laundered" through the organization’s front businesses.

---

## Laundering as an AI Logic Gate

Laundering is not an action performed by players; it is a **Background Process** managed by the Economy service:

* **Throughput:** Based on the number of "Front Businesses" assigned to an AI Organization (from `Organizations.md`), the Economy service automatically processes a percentage of `Illicit Funds` into `Operating Budget` over time.
* **Economic Thresholds:**
    * **Low Budget:** AI Director shifts focus to high-payout, low-cost scenarios to survive.
    * **High Budget:** AI Director unlocks complex, capital-intensive scenarios (e.g., arming lookouts with advanced gear, upgrading safehouse security).

---

## Read-Model API

The AI Director queries this service to make decisions based on the financial "health" of the organization.

```lua
local Economy = DCE:GetService("Economy")

-- AI Director checks if the Org can afford a high-tier event
if Economy.GetOperatingBudget(orgId) > highTierCost then
    AI.SetStrategy("Aggressive")
end

Integration: Intelligence Reporting (ERS)
Even though these are AI organizations, their financial status should still influence the ERS environment to provide police with "leads."

Anomaly Generation: When an AI Org's wealth crosses a specific threshold, the EconomyAdapter pushes a notification to the ERS MDT (e.g., "Suspicious financial activity detected at [Location]").

Investigative Depth: Detectives can "investigate" a front business. If they successfully shut it down, the Economy service sends an event to the AI Director to force the Organization’s budget to drop, effectively "starving" the AI of its operational capabilities.

Emitted Events
economy:ai:budget_updated — { orgId, newBudget }

economy:ai:laundering_event — { orgId, location, activityLevel }

What This Document Does Not Cover
Player banking systems (Out of scope for AI-only simulation).

Inventory management (AI organizations do not use physical items; they use weights and state variables).
